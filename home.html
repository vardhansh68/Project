Gourmet Experience

Focusing on the gourmet diner as well as the youth of India, The GreenYard Café aims to bring Indian cuisine back “in-Vogue”. “The GreenYard” can have many connotations, but at The GreenYard Café, it has just one, “creating an illusion” with its cuisine. Best described as a gourmet experience.




Vision

With a vision of bringing Indian Cuisine back “in-Vogue”, The GreenYard Café endeavours to showcase a unique, modernist approach to Indian food, where guests not only enjoy culinary innovations.




Modernist Cuisine

Flowing smoke, pebble like smooth spheres, table theatrics and culinary illusion are a few aspects which describe the The GreenYard Café experience.
